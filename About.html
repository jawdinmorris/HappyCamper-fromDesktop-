About Us

Happy Campers.. It's In The Name

Happy Campers was created by Jordan Morris in late 2017 to help Australians find where best to spend the limited time they have. Whether you're looking to travel across Australia for the next 17 years or you're just looking for a chill sunday sesh Happy Campers can help you find your perfect destination.


We Know It's Good Because You Know It's Good

With zero reviews from no Campers we have the largest collection of user-submitted reviews for Camp Site, Caravan Site, Waterhole and National Park locations. All reviews are filtered based on feedback from other users and your own previous experiences; so you can spend less time planning and more time being an All Aussie Adventurer.
